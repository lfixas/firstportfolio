About Me

For almost my entire life I have been intrigued by computers, how they work, how people do this or that. But I never had the courage to take the plunge into wanting to do tech for fear of not understanding anything.
In the end, at the age of 21, I decided to do what I really like, instead of doing what people wanted me to like.
And here I am in Pre-MSc Pro at Epitech Strasbourg in order to finally know how to deal with this whole world which was until then austere to me. I hope to see globally all the technologies that exist, or at least know that they exist.
So my goal is to become a Full-Stack developer in the future.
